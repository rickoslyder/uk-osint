About UK OSINT Nexus

UK OSINT Nexus is a unified Open Source Intelligence tool that aggregates data from multiple free UK government APIs and databases.

Data Sources

  • Companies House - UK company registry
  • MOT History - Vehicle test records
  • BAILII - Legal case database
  • Contracts Finder - Government procurement
  • Charity Commission - Registered charities
  • FCA Register - Financial services firms
  • DVLA - Vehicle enquiry service
  • Electoral Commission - Political donations
  • Police Data - Street-level crime data

Features

  • Unified search across all sources
  • Automatic cross-source correlation
  • Export to JSON, CSV, Markdown, HTML
  • Entity relationship visualization
